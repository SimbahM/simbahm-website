Hard Surface 3D Design (Blender)

Apple iPhone 17
Pro Max

This 3D concept of the iPhone 17 Pro Max showcases a sleek, futuristic reinterpretation of Apple’s iconic design language. Created entirely in Blender using hard-surface modeling techniques, the concept emphasizes precision, form, and material realism.

The body features a bold metallic orange finish, blending elegance with a modern industrial edge. Smooth bevels and refined edges highlight the phone’s premium aesthetic, while the triple-lens camera module is seamlessly integrated into a raised, sculpted backplate for visual balance and functionality.

Advanced lighting and reflections accentuate the polished surfaces, giving the render a highly realistic and professional look. This concept represents a vision of next-generation Apple design—minimal, powerful, and artistically engineered.
