Kinterior
Brand Identity

A minimalist approach was used in the visual identity project for the interior design firm KINTERIOR in order to go hand in hand with the proposition that the company makes. The Kinterior brand is seen more soberly and exclusively to its very modern and serious appearance.. Attracting high class clients who are willing to work with the company.

I decided to build a neutral color palette with a subtle nod to interior design hues so that all the characteristics described in the briefing as "Power exclusivity and modernism" were represented.

New brand

The new identity's representation of progress and the future's objective calls for a modern, clutter-free, easily readable, and memorable logo.

Logomark

The K company’s initial served as the basis for the logo, which was created to symbolize strength, sobriety, and elegance. This makes perfect sense when expressing the interior design industry.

Simple geometric forms like the square and circle were used to establish a tone of solidity and sophistication. The symbol’s simplicity enhances its memorability and carries the elements' order along with it.

Golden Ration

The use of the golden ratio methodology making sure all parts for the logo are placed harmonically.
