NMBConnect

UI UX Casy Study.

Overview

NMBConnect is a universal financial services platform that provides seamless peer to peer transaction processing and a wide array of financial payments.

My Role

So I was commissioned by a local bank here is Zimbabwe Africa called NMB Bank. They recently launched their banking mobile application called "MNBConnect" and I had the honour of doing the visuals for the product and the face of the mobile app.

Project Goal

The key objective was to strike a compromise between an interface that was easy to understand and simple to use without detracting too much from the application's visual appeal. Both new and experienced users should have a clear grasp of the operation of the application.
