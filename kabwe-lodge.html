Logo Design

Kabwe Lodge
Logo Design

In Tonga, the word for stone is Kabwe. The stone is depicted as the logo’s symbol in the form of a diamond.

The vertical layout of the word-mark gives the brand a more distinctive and unusual appearance.

The royal brand will use a black and white color palette which creates an energetic, active feeling, making our eyes bounce around a space. However, there can be variations in contrast with black and white schemes too, with the tones in high-contrast schemes ranging from bright highlights to dark shadows.

The logo was designed using the carved diamond as its foundation and was intended to represent power, sobriety, and elegance. Expressing the business’s industry and showcasing its royalty.

The simplicity of the mark makes it easier to remember and carries the order of the elements with it.
