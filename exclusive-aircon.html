For this project I needed to focus on professionalism, structure and positioning in the market in order to provide a strong, timeless and representative solution.

The logomark is a simplified version of the old logo, decided to create a distinctive logomark that could work in any application needed. Sheped by the concept of aircon, air circulation. Furthermore, I combined it with Montserrat Typeface making it distinctive and readable at the same time.
