Project Overview

CABS Bank is one of Zimbabwe’s leading financial institutions, serving a wide range of retail and corporate customers. With the increasing adoption of mobile banking, the client identified the need to modernize their existing mobile application to better align with current digital trends, improve usability, and enhance the overall customer experience.

I was approached to redesign the existing CABS mobile banking app UI, focusing on delivering a clean, modern, user-friendly, and visually appealing interface that would improve customer engagement while maintaining the trust and reliability expected from a banking platform.

The final solution was delivered as a complete high-fidelity design in Figma, together with a scalable design system for future product growth.

Project Objectives

The goal of this project was to modernize the overall visual identity of the CABS mobile banking app while improving usability and clarity for everyday financial transactions. A key focus was placed on simplifying complex financial information to make it easier for users to understand and interact with. The project also aimed to ensure a consistent look and feel across all screens, while establishing a scalable design system that would support future updates and feature expansion.

Design Process

The project followed a structured UI design workflow to ensure quality and consistency throughout the product.

UI Research & Visual Exploration

I began by analyzing popular mobile banking applications both locally and internationally to understand current UI trends, accessibility standards, and best practices. This phase helped shape the visual direction and identify opportunities to improve clarity and usability.

Information Architecture & Layout Planning

With the key user journeys defined, I structured screen layouts to prioritize essential actions such as balance visibility, transactions, payments, and quick access features. The goal was to reduce cognitive load and make navigation effortless.
