Logo Design

Hope Wellness Brand Identity

The logo features a basic "H" that stands in for the Hope Wellness brand and a silhouette of two people speaking. The doctor and the patient are the two individuals this silhouette depicts.

The logo embodies the organization's desired mission of caring for its patients.

Golden Ratio

The use of the golden ratio methodology making sure all parts for the logo are placed harmonically.
