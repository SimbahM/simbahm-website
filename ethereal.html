A good trademark is always simple and minimal! People easily remember and identify.

For this project, I had to prioritize professionalism, structure, and market positioning in order to deliver a solution that is strong, timeless, and representative. The logo appears neat and easy to recall.

Also, I combined the symbol with Montserrat typeface for the project based on the symbol's details, making it both distinct and legible.
