Price Tag

Why Price tag icon?

Pricelyst is the name of the brand, which encourages customers to compare prices of goods at nearby retailers. I prefer to utilize a price tag icon because it is more effective and reflects the brand.

Simple logos are meaningless at first, but when used consistently with the name, they start to make sense and become memorable

Typography

I decided to use the Montserrat typeface, which produces the ideal logo lockup.
