Shop Savvy Brand Identity

A bold italic mark appears in the logo-mark. The major goal was to make the mark simple, clear, and modern. To achieve this, I shortened the final two words of the word SAVVY, which made the symbol more abstract and artistic.

The new design, it features Savvy in a larger italic font and Shop in a smaller font, gives the brand a more distinctive and unusual appearance.

Changing the font type to a more bold one give the new brand new and fresh modern look.

The logo-mark was constructed from the font Manrope Bold and Gotham Bold Italics.
