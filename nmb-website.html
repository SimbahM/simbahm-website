Overview

NMB Bank approached me to collaborate on a comprehensive redesign of their website, aiming to enhance user experience and modernize their digital presence. The project presented an exciting opportunity to translate their brand identity into a seamless and intuitive online platform. Recognizing the importance of user-centric design, the focus was placed on understanding the needs of NMB's diverse customer base, from individual clients to corporate partners.

Through in-depth research and collaborative workshops, we established key user journeys and identified areas for improvement within the existing website structure. The resulting design prioritized clear navigation, simplified information architecture, and a visually engaging interface, ultimately striving to create a digital experience that reflects NMB Bank's commitment to innovation and customer service.

My role

As the UI designer for this website project, my role was to translate the client's vision and user needs into a visually compelling and functionally intuitive digital experience. This involved a multi-faceted approach, beginning with a thorough understanding of the target audience and the website's core objectives. I conducted user research, created wireframes to define the website's structure and layout, and developed interactive prototypes to test usability.

My focus was on creating a seamless user flow, ensuring that information was easily accessible and that the website's visual elements aligned with the client's brand identity. This included designing the website's visual hierarchy, selecting appropriate typography and color palettes, and creating consistent UI elements. Ultimately, my goal was to craft a user interface that not only looked aesthetically pleasing but also provided a positive and efficient user experience, contributing to the website's overall success.

Process

User Journey Map

To ensure a user-centered design, I developed comprehensive User Journey Maps for the website. These maps visualized the various paths users would take, highlighting pain points and opportunities for improvement, ultimately informing key design decisions.

Information Architecture

To lay a strong foundation for the website design, I focused on crafting a robust information architecture. This involved a deep dive into the website's content, identifying key user tasks, and organizing the information into a clear and intuitive structure. Through meticulous analysis and strategic planning, I developed a sitemap and navigation system that prioritized user needs, leading to a more efficient and satisfying browsing experience.

Mind Map

I employed mind mapping as a crucial tool for visualizing and organizing the website's information architecture. This involved creating a central node representing the core purpose of the website, branching out to encompass key features, user journeys, and content categories.

This visual representation allowed for a comprehensive understanding of the website's scope and interconnected elements, facilitating the identification of logical navigation pathways and potential user flow challenges. The mind map served as a foundational blueprint, ensuring that all aspects of the website were considered and strategically placed, ultimately contributing to a more cohesive and user-friendly design.

Wireframes

During the website design project, I played a key role in the initial planning stages by creating detailed wireframes. These wireframes served as the blueprint for the site's layout, ensuring a clear understanding of the user flow and information architecture before visual design began. This process allowed for early identification and resolution of potential usability issues, ultimately contributing to a more efficient and effective design process.

Final Design

The design adheres to a clean, modern, and approachable aesthetic. I prioritized clarity and usability, ensuring that information is easily digestible and navigation is intuitive. The visual language utilizes a vibrant color palette, friendly typography, and engaging imagery to create a welcoming and motivating atmosphere.
