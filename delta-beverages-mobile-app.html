Case Study

Delta Beverages Mobile App - UI & UX Design

Project Type:

Mobile App Redesign

My Role:

UI UX Designer

Design Tools:

Figma, Adobe Illustrator & Photoshop

Deliverables:

High-fidelity UI screens, Interactive Prototype, Design System
Project Overview

Delta Beverages, one of Zimbabwe’s leading beverage companies, approached me to design a modern mobile application aimed at improving customer engagement and streamlining access to their services and product information. The goal of the project was to create a user-friendly, visually engaging, and scalable mobile experience that aligns with the brand’s identity while delivering real business value.

The Challenge

The challenge was to design an intuitive mobile experience that could serve multiple user needs—ranging from product exploration and brand discovery to customer interaction and service access—while maintaining performance, simplicity, and visual consistency across the app.

My Role

I was responsible for the entire UI and UX design process, from research and ideation to final high-fidelity designs and prototyping. This included:

The goal was simple but challenging: take complex financial transactions and make them feel effortless, secure, and modern.

✅

User research and experience planning

✅

Information architecture and user flow design

✅

Wireframing and interaction design

✅

High-fidelity UI design

✅

Prototyping and usability validation

UX Process

The design process began with understanding Delta Beverages’ business goals and identifying core user needs. I mapped out the main user journeys to ensure smooth navigation and logical task flow. Wireframes were created to establish layout structure and prioritize key content before moving into visual design.

The focus was on:

✅

Simplifying complex user journeys

✅

Reducing friction in navigation

✅

Ensuring accessibility and ease of use

✅

Designing for speed and clarity in everyday interactions

UI Process

The visual design reflects Delta Beverages’ strong brand presence through clean layouts, bold typography, and consistent use of brand colors. The interface was designed to feel modern, energetic, and trustworthy—matching the brand’s market position.

Key UI highlights include:

✅

A clean, intuitive home screen

✅

Clear visual hierarchy for content

✅

Consistent iconography and components

✅

A responsive and scalable design system for future updates

Outcome

The final deliverable was a fully interactive, high-fidelity mobile app design submitted in Figma, complete with a comprehensive design system. The result is a modern, user-centered mobile experience built to support Delta Beverages’ digital growth and future feature expansion.

Key Takeaway

This project strengthened my ability to design end-to-end mobile experiences that balance business objectives with real user needs—while maintaining strong brand consistency and visual appeal.
