Introduction

NMBConnect is a universal financial services platform that provides seamless peer to peer transaction processing and a wide array of financial payments.

My role

As a UI designer on this mobile app project, my role encompassed translating user needs and business goals into a visually compelling and functionally effective interface.

Problem

NMB Bank's existing mobile application presented inconsistencies in user experience and visual design, failing to fully align with the bank's established brand guidelines and strategic design goals. This resulted in a fragmented user journey across different platforms, hindering brand recognition and potentially impacting customer trust. Specifically, the lack of a unified design system across iOS and Android platforms created visual discrepancies and usability issues, leading to a disjointed user experience. Therefore, the core problem addressed in this redesign was the need to create a cohesive and brand-aligned mobile application that delivers a consistent and intuitive user experience across all platforms, effectively reinforcing NMB Bank's brand identity and enhancing customer engagement.

Goals

In designing the mobile banking app, I prioritized a user-centered approach that meticulously balanced the bank's strategic business goals with the personal needs and aspirations of its users. This involved a deep understanding of both perspectives: for the bank, goals like increased customer engagement, reduced operational costs, and enhanced brand reputation were paramount. Simultaneously, I recognized that users sought convenience, security, and intuitive functionality that empowered them to manage their finances effectively.

To achieve this balance, I employed a design process that integrated user research and stakeholder feedback at every stage. Wireframes and prototypes were iteratively refined, ensuring that each feature not only served a clear business objective but also addressed a specific user need. By focusing on creating a seamless and efficient user experience, I aimed to drive user adoption and engagement, which in turn contributed to the bank's overall success. This approach ensured that the final product was not only a functional tool but also a valuable asset that fostered a strong, mutually beneficial relationship between the bank and its customers.

Process

We leveraged a design sprint to efficiently design and validate core features for the banking mobile app. This rapid prototyping and testing process ensured a user-centered approach and accelerated the app's development.

Wireframes

I developed low-fidelity and mid-fidelity wireframes for the banking mobile app, utilizing industry-standard tools to map out user flows, screen layouts, and interactive elements. These wireframes were critical in establishing the app's information hierarchy and ensuring a seamless user experience.

Final Design

The culmination of the UI/UX journey manifested in a final design that prioritized both functionality and aesthetic appeal. Through iterative prototyping and rigorous user testing, the initial concepts were refined, resulting in an interface that addressed key user pain points and enhanced overall usability.

By focusing on user-centered design principles, the final UI/UX delivered a product that not only met but exceeded user expectations, resulting in increased engagement and satisfaction.
