Apexbytes

Logo Design Project

Project

The client approached me with a request to refine the logo by updating the typeface. They wanted a fresh look that better aligns with their brand identity while maintaining recognizability.

My goal was to enhance readability, modernize the design, and ensure consistency across various platforms.

Apexbytes Old Logo

Logo Typeface

I have redesigned the logo by updating the typography to the Montserrat font. This change enhances readability, gives the logo a more modern and clean look, and ensures better scalability across different platforms.

In addition to changing the typeface of the logo, I also refined the symbol to achieve a cleaner and more polished look.

The icon looks great! Just a few areas need attention, particularly where parts overlap and the asymmetrical elements of the symbol.

I redrew the symbol using straight geometric lines to achieve a cleaner and more symmetrical look.

The final look of the symbol is cleaner and more modern compared to before.
