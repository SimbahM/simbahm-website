The Design Challenge

Design is evolving and becoming simpler, more collaborative, and more potent. By creating symbols and marks that reflect the powerful simplicity and harmony of what they represent, we can adapt to these movements and become part of them.

For this project, I seek to create a geometric piece joining different pieces and forming a conceptual abstract letter L. With it, I seek to create a very powerful visual language that speaks the same language of brand values.

Minimalism. Geometry. Esthetic.
